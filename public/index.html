How to Get Your SoundCloud Token

After clicking "Save Config", your token is saved and you won't need to do this again — unless it expires occasionally.
1 Log In to SoundCloud
Use the SoundCloud window that opened in your browser and log in to your account.
2 Open DevTools
Press Cmd + Option + I on Mac to open the browser Developer Tools panel.
3 Go to the Storage Tab
Click Storage at the top of the DevTools panel. If you're using Chrome, look for Application instead.
4 Click Cookies
In the left sidebar, click Cookies to expand it.
5 Select soundcloud.com
Under Cookies, click on https://soundcloud.com to see the cookies for that site.
6 Filter for Your Token
Use the filter bar at the top and type oauth to narrow down the results.
7 Find Your Token
Look for a value starting with 2- followed by numbers and letters.
2-123456-789012345-AbCdEfGhIjKlMnOp
8 Copy the Value
Click the cookie row and copy the full value from the Value column.
9 Paste It In
Close this guide and paste your token into the SoundCloud Auth Token field.

How to Use — Apple Music

Make sure Apple Music is open on your Mac before starting a sync.
1 Get Your SoundCloud Token
Click "How to Get?" next to the token field for a full walkthrough. You only need to do this once — your token is saved after clicking Save Config.
2 Paste Your Playlist URL
Copy the direct link to your SoundCloud playlist and paste it in the Playlist URL field.
3 Choose a Download Folder
Click Browse and create a new folder for these songs. Each playlist must have its own folder — never reuse an existing one.
4 Click Start Sync
Watch the terminal output for progress. Songs will be downloaded and added to your Apple Music library automatically.
5 Sync to iPhone and iPad
To sync automatically to your other devices, open Apple Music, go to Music, then Settings, then General, and turn on Sync Library. Songs will appear on your iPhone and iPad within a few minutes.

How to Use — Spotify

Make sure the Spotify desktop app is installed and open on your Mac before starting a sync.
1 Get Your SoundCloud Token
Click "How to Get?" next to the token field for a full walkthrough. You only need to do this once — your token is saved after clicking Save Config.
2 Paste Your Playlist URL
Copy the direct link to your SoundCloud playlist and paste it in the Playlist URL field.
3 Choose a Download Folder
Click Browse and select or create a folder for these songs. This folder will be added as a Local Files source in Spotify.
4 Click Start Sync
Songs will be downloaded to your folder and Spotify will open automatically.
5 Enable Local Files in Spotify
In Spotify, click your profile picture, then Settings. Scroll to Your Library and toggle on Show Local Files. Under Show songs from, click Add Source and select the folder you downloaded your songs into.
6 Add Songs to a Playlist
Go to Your Library, then Local Files in Spotify. Select your songs and add them to any existing or new playlist.
7 iPhone — Enable Local Files
On your iPhone open Spotify, tap your profile picture, then Settings and Privacy, then Apps and Devices, and toggle on Local audio files.
8 iPhone — Transfer via USB
Plug your iPhone into your Mac with a USB cable. Open Finder and select your iPhone in the sidebar. Find the Spotify folder and drag your downloaded songs into it. They will appear in Spotify on your iPhone under Your Library, then Local Files.
9 iPhone — Transfer Without USB
On your Mac, copy your downloaded music files in Finder. On your iPhone open the Files app, go to Browse, then On My iPhone, tap the Spotify folder, and paste the files there. They will appear in Spotify under Local Files.
